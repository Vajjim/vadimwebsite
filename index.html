About Me

Academic & Ecological Focus

Currently pursuing a BSc in Ecological and Environmental Science with Management at the University of Edinburgh. I’m particularly interested in biodiversity, conservation, and sustainable resource use. My coursework covers environmental impact assessment, biological processes, soil, water and atmospheric processes, risk management, and conservation strategies, alongside an emphasis on business and management aspects such as finance, stakeholder engagement, strategic planning, and decision-making.
